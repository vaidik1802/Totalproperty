TOTAL CONSTRUCTORS

COMPANY OVERVIEW

Total Constructors is a professional construction and project support company committed to delivering dependable, high-quality, and efficient construction solutions for residential, commercial, renovation, restoration, redevelopment, and property improvement projects. As part of Total Property Solutions Inc., Total Constructors provides clients with a trusted construction partner capable of handling projects with professionalism, safety, quality workmanship, and practical project coordination.

Our company is built on the understanding that every construction project requires clear planning, skilled execution, safety-focused practices, quality control, and dependable service delivery. Whether supporting small property improvements, structural upgrades, renovation-related works, tenant improvements, general contracting assistance, or site-related project work, Total Constructors provides practical, efficient, and reliable solutions tailored to each client's needs.

We proudly serve homeowners, property managers, landlords, developers, commercial operators, contractors, and institutional clients by offering construction support that emphasizes quality, professionalism, accountability, and timely execution.

MISSION

Our mission is to deliver safe, reliable, and high-quality construction solutions that help clients improve, restore, build, and maintain their properties with confidence. We aim to create long-term value through professional workmanship, strong project coordination, transparent communication, and consistent service excellence.

VISION

Our vision is to become a trusted and recognized construction support company known for quality delivery, professional standards, safety leadership, and dependable project execution across residential and commercial sectors.

CORE VALUES

  • Safety First – Protecting people, property, and project environments at all times
  • Quality Workmanship – Delivering reliable and professionally executed work
  • Integrity – Building trust through honesty, accountability, and transparency
  • Efficiency – Completing projects with practical planning and organized execution
  • Professionalism – Maintaining strong communication, site conduct, and project standards
  • Client Commitment – Prioritizing customer satisfaction and project success

SERVICES & CORE ACTIVITIES

GENERAL CONSTRUCTION SERVICES

Activities include general construction support, small to medium-scale building projects, residential and commercial construction works, property improvement projects, building upgrades and modifications, construction labor and site support, project coordination, tenant improvement support, and general contracting assistance.

RENOVATION & REMODELING WORKS

Activities include interior renovation works, exterior property improvements, basement finishing support, kitchen renovation support, bathroom renovation support, office and retail upgrades, wall modifications and framing support, flooring and finishing preparation, ceiling and partition works, and property modernization projects.

FRAMING & STRUCTURAL SUPPORT

Activities include wood framing support, interior partition framing, non-load-bearing wall construction, room additions and reconfiguration support, structural preparation works, construction layout support, and basic carpentry support.

EXTERIOR CONSTRUCTION & PROPERTY IMPROVEMENT

Activities include exterior repair and improvement support, site cleanup and construction area preparation, fencing and basic exterior installations, outdoor structure support, exterior finishing support, and entrance/access improvement works.

WHY CHOOSE TOTAL CONSTRUCTORS

Clients choose Total Constructors because we focus on delivering practical, dependable, and professionally managed construction support solutions.

We are committed to safe work practices, quality workmanship, professional project coordination, responsive communication, efficient job execution, clean and organized work environments, client-focused service delivery, and reliable support for residential and commercial needs.

As part of Total Property Solutions Inc., we also understand the value of integrated project support, allowing us to coordinate effectively with related services such as demolition, restoration, environmental services, waste management, and moving support where needed.

HEALTH & SAFETY PLAN

At Total Constructors, health and safety is a fundamental part of our operations. We are committed to maintaining a work environment that prioritizes the safety of our workers, clients, subcontractors, site visitors, and the public.

Our safety practices include pre-job hazard assessments, site-specific safety planning, PPE usage, safe tool and equipment handling, housekeeping and site cleanliness, ladder and access safety, slip/trip/fall prevention, manual handling awareness, and emergency preparedness procedures.

We encourage all workers and team members to actively participate in creating a safe work environment. Safety is not treated as a checklist item — it is a daily operational responsibility and an essential part of our company culture.

QUALITY ASSURANCE & QUALITY CONTROL PLAN

Total Constructors is committed to delivering construction services with a strong focus on quality, consistency, workmanship, and client satisfaction.

Our quality management approach is intended to deliver work that meets project expectations, maintain consistency in workmanship, reduce rework, improve project efficiency, and support professional service standards across all project types.

Our quality control practices include scope review before project commencement, clear work planning and task coordination, material and work area preparation checks, ongoing inspections during project execution, attention to detail in finishing stages, final walkthroughs, and continuous improvement based on feedback and project experience.

WORKFORCE & TRAINING COMMITMENT

At Total Constructors, we recognize that project quality and safety depend heavily on the professionalism and preparedness of the people performing the work. We are committed to maintaining a workforce culture that values site professionalism, safety awareness, workmanship standards, reliability, accountability, respect for client property, and continuous practical learning and improvement.

COMMITMENT TO CLIENTS

At Total Constructors, we understand that clients need more than just labor — they need a construction partner they can trust to deliver work responsibly, professionally, and efficiently. Our commitment is to support every project with a focus on safety, quality, reliability, clear communication, practical problem-solving, and professional execution.
